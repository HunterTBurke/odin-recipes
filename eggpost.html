Best Post-Workout Meal

Description

Sunny-side eggs with runny yolks atop wilted spinach, paired with crispy, cheesy potatoes and a slice of buttered sourdough toast topped with creamy avocado. Served with cold milk for a savory, satisfying recovery bite.

Ingredients

    4 large eggs
    1/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese
    1 cup fresh spinach
    1/2 small potato (75g), shredded or diced
    1/4 medium avocado (50g)
    2 tablespoons butter (1 for potatoes/eggs, 1 for toast)
    No added salt, pepper only
    1 slice sourdough bread (50g)
    1 cup whole milk

Steps

    1. Heat 1 tablespoon butter in a skillet over medium heat, add shredded potato, and cook for 5-6 minutes until golden.
    2. Sprinkle cheddar cheese over potatoes, let melt for 1 minute, then set aside on a plate.
    3. In the same skillet, add spinach and cook for 1-2 minutes until wilted, then push to one side.
    4. Crack 4 eggs into the skillet, cook sunny-side up for 3-4 minutes until whites are set but yolks remain runny.
    5. Toast sourdough, spread with 1 tablespoon butter, and top with sliced avocado.
    6. Serve eggs with spinach and cheesy potatoes, alongside avocado butter toast and a glass of milk.
    7. Cleanup: Rinse skillet and utensils (2 minutes).
