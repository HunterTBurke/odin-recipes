Best Salmon Dinner

Description

Juicy salmon seared in butter, paired with creamy mashed sweet potato and steamed broccoli and spinach. Finished with a glass of milk for a rich, earthy, and slightly sweet dinner.

Ingredients

    10 oz salmon fillet
    1 large sweet potato (200g), microwaved and mashed
    2 cups broccoli florets
    2 tablespoons butter (1 for salmon, 1 for sweet potato)
    1 cup fresh spinach
    No added salt, pepper only
    1 cup whole milk

Steps

    1. Microwave sweet potato (pierce, cook 5-7 minutes).
    2. Steam broccoli and spinach together in a pot with 1 inch water for 5 minutes (overlaps).
    3. Sear salmon in 1 tablespoon butter for 4-5 minutes per side.
    4. Mash sweet potato with 1 tablespoon butter.
    5. Serve with milk.
