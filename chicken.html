Best Chicken recipe

Description

Tender chicken strips stir-fried with vibrant broccoli, carrots, and bell peppers in a light coconut aminos sauce, served over fluffy jasmine rice. A flavorful, balanced midday meal.

Ingredients

    10 oz chicken breast, sliced
    2 cups cooked jasmine rice (~1 cup dry)
    2 cups mixed veggies (broccoli, carrots, bell peppers)
    2 tablespoons olive oil
    1 tablespoon coconut aminos
    1 teaspoon minced garlic
    1 teaspoon grated ginger

Steps

    1. Cook rice: Boil 1 cup dry jasmine rice in 2 cups water for 10-12 minutes (overlaps).
    2. Heat 1 tablespoon olive oil, cook chicken 5-7 minutes, remove.
    3. Add 1 tablespoon olive oil, stir-fry veggies, garlic, and ginger for 4-5 minutes.
    4. Return chicken, add coconut aminos, stir 2 minutes.
    5. Serve over rice.(3 minutes).
