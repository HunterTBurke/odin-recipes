Best Chicken recipe

Description

A colorful stir-fry with golden chicken pieces and vibrant vegetables (red and yellow bell peppers, green broccoli, orange carrots) in a glossy, savory sauce, garnished with chopped green onions, served on a white plate. The recipe’s estimated protein content (25 g per serving) is based on standard nutritional values for chicken (4 oz = 25 g protein), with additional nutrients from vegetables. The use of olive oil ensures compliance with the user’s oil preference, and the quick cooking time (20 minutes) fits the user’s time constraint, including setup (finding ingredients, chopping).

Ingredients

    - 2 tbsp. extra virgin olive oil
    - 1 lb. chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
    - Salt and pepper to taste
    - 1 red bell pepper, sliced
    - 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
    - 1 small head broccoli, cut into florets
    - 2 carrots, peeled and sliced
    - 3 garlic cloves, minced
    - 1 tsp. grated ginger
    - 1/4 cup low-sodium soy sauce
    - 2 tbsp. honey
    - Juice of 1 lime
    - Chopped green onions, for serving

Steps

    1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
    2. Season chicken with salt and pepper, cook until golden, about 5 minutes. Transfer to a plate.
    3. Add bell peppers, broccoli, and carrots, cook until slightly tender, about 5 minutes.
    4. Stir in garlic and ginger until fragrant, about 1 minute.
    5. Return chicken, add soy sauce, honey, and lime juice, cook until sauce thickens, about 2 minutes.
    6. Serve garnished with green onions.
