Best Chicken recipe

Description

A colorful stir-fry with golden chicken pieces and vibrant vegetables (red and yellow bell peppers, green broccoli, orange carrots) in a glossy, savory sauce, garnished with chopped green onions, served on a white plate. The recipe’s estimated protein content is 76g, with additional nutrients from vegetables. The use of olive oil ensures compliance with the user’s oil preference, and the quick cooking time (18 minutes) fits the user’s time constraint, including setup (finding ingredients, chopping).

Ingredients

    10 oz chicken breast, sliced
    2 cups cooked jasmine rice (~1 cup dry)
    2 cups mixed veggies (broccoli, carrots, bell peppers)
    2 tablespoons olive oil
    1 tablespoon coconut aminos
    1 teaspoon minced garlic
    1 teaspoon grated ginger

Steps

    1. Cook rice: Boil 1 cup dry jasmine rice in 2 cups water for 10-12 minutes (overlaps).
    2. Heat 1 tablespoon olive oil, cook chicken 5-7 minutes, remove.
    3. Add 1 tablespoon olive oil, stir-fry veggies, garlic, and ginger for 4-5 minutes.
    4. Return chicken, add coconut aminos, stir 2 minutes.
    5. Serve over rice.(3 minutes).
